The following commands shall be run in your VS Code terminal.

*** Creating Virtual Enviroment ***

python -m venv <My_Virtual_Enviroment>

*** Activating Virtual Enviroment ***

My_Virtual_Enviroment\Scripts\activate


*** Installing Packages into a Virtual Enviroment ***

My_Virtual_Enviroment\pip install <package name>


*** Installing NetworkX with its complete functionality ***

My_Virtual_Enviroment\pip install networkx[all]
